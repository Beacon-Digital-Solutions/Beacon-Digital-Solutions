Our approach to your data

Your data is yours. It does not belong to us. Our tools are designed so that your personal and financial information stays on your devices and in your own private iCloud. We do not collect it, we do not see it, and we do not ask you to send it to us.

Any future features that provide additional assistance will be built to operate on your own devices and in your own iCloud whenever possible. This is a deliberate choice to protect the people who use our tools.
